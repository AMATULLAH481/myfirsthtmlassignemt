Sub-divisions, how to read for fun and beginners recommendations

Reading can be such a bore sometimes, and this is coming from a bookworm. To know your type of genre you need to begin this journey with lots of patience because you have explore the genres you think you would be interested in and those you think you would not enjoy.

Genres and recommended authors for beginners

FANTASY
This is a genre that deals with the unrealistic realms of life like; magic ,elfs, angels , demons, elfs etc. It can be sub-divided into two(high fantasy; that does not deal with earth) and (low fantasy; that deals with earth while still being fantasy )

Famous authors under this category are ;

  1. J.K Rowling
  2. J.R.R. Tolkien
  3. Sarah.J.Maas
  4. Leigh Bardugo
THRILLER
This is usually a play centered on an exciting plot typically dealing with crime or espionage(spying)

Famous authors under this category are ;

  1. Sydney Sheldon
  2. Dan Brown
  3. Lee Child
  4. David Baldacci
  5. John Grisham
  6. Gillian Flynn
MYSTERY
This form of literature deals with a plot that makes the reader think about a crime investigation, solve a puzzle , most times concerned with a murder .Most people entwine mystery and thriller and the stark difference is in mystery novels a crime is revealed and there has to be flashback for it to be solved ; while in thrillers , the bad guy is often known from the start and has to be chased like Thanos.....

Famous authors in this "mysterious" field are

  1. Agatha
  2. Gillian Flynn
  3. John Grisham
  4. JD Robb
ROMANCE (some of you all have been waiting)
This genre mainly focuses on an adventure of two or more individuals in which the main character is called to show chivalry or love. Not surprisingly , Romance as a genre has more than 3 sub-genres a few are (Historical romance, paranormal romance, romantic comedy.)

Famous authors in this field are

  1. Jane Auten
  2. Nora Roberts
  3. Nicholas Sparks (no pun intended)
  4. Debbie Macomber
  5. Danielle Steel
  6. Jasmine Guillory

It should be noted that these authors are not supposedly the best in their fields because when it comes to books "the best" acts a relative statement. Hence, if you are noy enjoying a book , drop it and take another one and note reading reading frequently builds slowly so do not beat yourself for not reading in a year or a month Everyone is a bookworm as long as they find joy in reading whether fictional or not.
